Home Page Wireframe:

This is the initial wireframe for the home page. It uses parallax backgrounds that enhances the visual appeal of the page by creating a sense of depth and movement. This idea is later revised and discarded in favour of regular background images. In the centre, a stroke animation plays to capture attention and intrigue of the audience

Memories:

The memories page was initially planned to be the history page. The layout would take a similar approach but would use images instead of videos. Each image would have shown a crucial piece of history about the club

Wireframes:

The wireframe page uses card sliders to display each wireframe in a neat and organised manner. User's can view each wireframe with its respective explanation and cycle through the various cards with the next and previous arrows
