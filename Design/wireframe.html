Home Page Wireframe:

This is the initial wireframe for the home page. It uses parallax backgrounds that enhances the visual appeal of the page by creating a sense of depth and movement. This idea is later revised and discarded in favour of regular background images. In the centre, a stroke animation plays to capture attention and intrigue of the audience

Memories:

The memories page was initially planned to be the history page. The layout would take a similar approach but would use images instead of videos. Each image would have shown a crucial piece of history about the club

Wireframes:

The wireframe page uses card sliders to display each wireframe in a neat and organised manner. User's can view each wireframe with its respective explanation and cycle through the various cards with the next and previous arrows

Data Visualizations Planning:

Initally I thought both data visualization piece could be placed on one page for convenience or perhaps they would relate to each other. However this was revised to give each piece it's own page to provide ample space to effectively translate the dataset into a visualization that could be fleshed out.

History:

A history section was going to be a second additional page after the memories page. Each image would represent an important moment in the club's history and would have an animated arrow that would play on scroll and would lead to the next milestone. I decided to not do this for this submission due to time constraints but it is still on the table for the exam submission

Form:

This form acts as a registration form for users to complete by filling in the required fields. In the context of this website, users can fill in their details to become a member or have an AC Milan fan account. I chose a simplistic design that effectively communicates the necessary fields. The form takes the centre of the page and therefore becomes the focus.
